News?

From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-09 15:37:34 UTC
I had a great suggestion from another boxer. What about a news segment?

He pointed out that this is a great way for someone to contribute who
may not have the means, expertise, time, money, etc. to contribute by
writing or submitting a news item and having someone who can record it
read it do the voicework.

I can envision people who may want to tell about an upcoming
gathering, or tell about a special event or box that is coming up.
Stuff that people put on blogs and make post about. There are always
special projects people are doing...like letterboxes for the troops,
or for Steve Irwin or those memorial type boxes. Or maybe an
announcement about a flood that wiped out a hundred boxes in some
area. I think there is a lot of potential for a segment like this,
and it would be a way for people to contribute in an off air kind of way.

What do you all think?

jackbear
