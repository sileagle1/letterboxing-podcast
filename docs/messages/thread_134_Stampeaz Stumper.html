From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-02-27 19:14:08 UTC
I have exciting news!

Stampeaz (and owner Webfoot) would like to sponsor the Letterpod. I
thought a good way to provide sponsorship would be in the form of a
game/contest.

Think of this like Car Talk's "Puzzler".

My initial thoughts is this could be some form of riddle, trivia,
puzzle, etc. that would be presented each show. Listeners could
respond by entering their answer into a virtual letterbox (passkey).
Sort of like what I did in the previous contest. The winners would
win a prize, which I am working with Webfoot on right now, perhaps a
gift certificate or some product(s)(PZ-Kut?). We'll work that out.

In return, we promote Stampeaz...give them a little plug, mention the
website, put a link on our website.

What do you all think? I know we as letterboxers tend to frown on
commercial endorsements in letterboxing, but this is a lot different
from JEEP or Garmin, and the other stuff geocachers do. This is one
of our own. Webfoot also has a official link from Atlas Quest, so I
don't think we are making some precedent here. Also, I'm not taking
money here, we are passing along prizes from Stampeaz, and in return
calling the game after them, and referring listeners to this service.

Please email me if you think I'm missing the point here, but I think
this could be a very fun segment for the show. I know there are a lot
of letterboxers out there who like the "mystery" box, I know I do.
This can hone those mental puzzle skills.

So, here's what I need from you:
1) Tell me if you like this idea or not.
2) Tell me an idea you have for the first "stumper". A trivia
question, a riddle, puzzle, cipher, audio daily double? Remember,
this is "radio", so we CAN do something with audio. I want them to be
challenging too, especially if they are getting a nice prize.

jackbear
