Need Volunteer for Intro...

From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-09 18:45:55 UTC
I need someone to volunteer to do the intro for the next episode...all
you need is a phone, and to email me for the password. This is what
we we need:

Each episode of the podcast begins with "Hi, this is (trailname) from
(place) and your listening to the Letterpod." This is a way to give a
shout-out to your hometown and boxers who might know you, or who
don't, but always wondered what you sounded like! You can record an
intro by calling a free recording service, called Gabcast. We have our
own area we can record to, and all you need is a telephone! First,
email jackbear and let him know you want the password to record an
intro for Letterpod. Then call 1-800-749-0632. Enter the Letterpod
channel number which is 11349 #. Then you enter the password and then
hit #. Then the voice prompts will tell you to press 1 to record a
message. At the tone, record your intro and hit # when you are done.
You can listen to it back, and change it if you goofed up. The message
gets automatically saved, and it will be available to use in a future
episode. Its easy! Just talk clearly and slowly enough that we can
hear you well.

jackbear
