I know this isn't letterbox related IPod news, but my local library consortium started a
downloadable audio books program yesterday. I haven't used it yet but thought it was
another great use of the IPod.

This may interest Choi and others, who's libraries are part of the Merrimack Valley Library
Consortium (www.mvlc.com). I guess you download the free software program and then
you get to download books on CD. Seems to be a pretty good selection. Another great
selling point for owning these awesome video/audio companions!

Take care, Orion/Kathie
P.S. This will definitely not stop me from visiting one of my favorite places-the library!
