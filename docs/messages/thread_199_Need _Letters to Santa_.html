When I was a kid, we used to write our letter to Santa and my mom would send it to the North Pole, which was the local radio station where "Santa" would read the letters on the air at 7 p.m. each night leading up to Christmas. I would sit by the radio patiently waiting for Santa to say, "Now here is a letter from John, and he wants a new snowspeeder, walkie talkies, and a pac-man video game" (dating myself here).

It was great fun....sooooooo....I WANT YOUR LETTERS TO SANTA with all your letterboxing hopes and desires for Christmas. I will select some for Santa to read on the December edition of the Letterpod. To sweeten the deal, I will also be selecting three folks that submit lists/letters at random to receive a Letterpod patch!

So, think about what you want for Christmas from Santa, and drop me a line on AQ, and I'll pass it along to him!
