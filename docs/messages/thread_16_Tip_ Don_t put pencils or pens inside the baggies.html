it has come to my attention once again the importance of keeping the
pens out of ziploc bags that may be in any letterbox. pens in logbook
bags or even stamp/ink pad bags, are the leading cause of holes in
these bags. if for some reason the pen is placed into a logbook bag,
cause holes and then the box becomes damaged, by say water, the
logbook can be lost.
