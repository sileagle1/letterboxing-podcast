New Feedback Page

From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-07-01 20:38:55 UTC
I have added a new page to the Letterpod website:

http://letterpod.podbean.com/feedback/

This will hopefully help facilitate more feedback and interaction with
our listeners.

I especially want to bring to your attention a new way you can
participate in the letterpod....Letterpod Voice Mail!

Voicemail - You can leave an audio comment, suggestion, question,
complaint :-( or anything else on our TOLL FREE voice mail. Just call
our Gabcast line at 1-800-749-0632 and enter in the Channel Number:
21082. Then enter the password MYPOD (69793) and you will be prompted
to record your message. Letterpod might even air your comment in a
future episode! Please remember to leave your trail name on the
message. For example, "Hi this is jackbear, and I think that the
Letterpod is the most fun you can have with an iPod!"

jackbear
