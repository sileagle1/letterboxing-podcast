Tales From the Trails

From: kathiekelleher (kathiekelleher@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-01 22:00:26 UTC
I finally found some breathing room and recorded my first Tales from the Trails. What fun!
I kind of forgot how to create tracks in GarageBand but it was like getting back on a bike.
No problem.

I recorded my audio directly to my iPod via an attached Micromemo mic. The recording
mode comes up automatically and you just click once and you're good to go. The quality is
outstanding. Then I uploaded the recordings to iTunes. After opening my GarageBand file,
I just dragged the iTune file onto the track I was working on. It was so easy!

I just wanted to share this with folks who might be considering making a podcast. The
Micromemo is a godsend and makes it so easy (no, I don't work for them...).

JackBear, I sent the file by way of yousendit.com. Please let me know if you didn't get it.
That's a great site :-)

Take care, Orion
P.S. Installment 2 is just about done and will be sent shortly.
