New Letterpod Patch Design Competition

From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-04 18:43:36 UTC
I'm very excited to announce a design competition for a new Letterpod patch. With the old patches are almost gone, I would like to launch the new design at this year's LB CON 2009.

The Prize: The winning designer will win 1) the final patch they design 2) the 4 CD compilation of Episodes 1-14 3) and will be interviewed for an upcoming episode of the Letterpod. Cool enough?

Requirements:
- All designs must be submitted to jackbear by midnight of May 25th, 2009
- designs should be submitted in .jpg or .pdf form please.
- patch design should not exceed 3x3 inches.
- designs should include the colors "purple and green" but are not limited to only these colors
- designs should include a pair of headphones, but are not limited to only headphones.

Design Selection:
Design submissions will be posted online, and voted upon by the letterboxing community. The design with the highest number of votes after a week will be the winning patch design! Patches will then be ordered, and launched at LB CON 2009.

Contact me with any questions!
Thanks and good luck!
