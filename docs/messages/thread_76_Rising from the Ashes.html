OK, so looks like we have some interest here to get things going, if
Choi wants to pass the baton on, I'm willing to pick it up.

I've talked a little to Orion and Lockwench offline and they are
interesting in putting some content together. I'll compile it as we
need, and get it posted somewhere. Right now I'm looking at some
differnt hosts, first on my list is www.podbean.com. We can start for
free, and then pay in once we get rolling. They have lots of features
that might work for us, like having multiple contributers, and estore
options (so we can sell a patch or something to offset the fees).

For the time being, lets maybe shoot for a seasonal timeframe. A
summer episode, fall...etc.

Orion has a plan for a great segment (hope I'm not letting the cat out
of the bag here) for "Tales from the Trail". These would be stories
from letterboxers about things they have encountered on the trail.
Lockwench also has a great idea about trying to get some clues on the
podcast, and I couldn't agree more. This might draw in some people
who wouldn't otherwise listen.

More to come...pipe in of you have an idea or want to record something
for the next episode.
