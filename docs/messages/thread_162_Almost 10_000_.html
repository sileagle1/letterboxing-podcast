Almost 10,000!

From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-07-17 14:18:29 UTC
The Letterpod website on podbean is closing in on it's 10,000th site
visit!

At the time this message was posted, we are at 9955.

This is kind of an odd number because Podbean keeps track of Site
Visitors...this is it's definition:
"Site Visitors, as it's called, gives the number of visitors to your
podcast site. To prevent any type of fake or redundant data, any
number of visitors with the same IP address will be counted only once
in a 24-hour period."

So it doesn't mean 10,000 different computers have come to Letterpod,
nor does it mean the total number of times the site has been visited.
Something in between.

I want to thank you all for making this podcast so successful and
making it to this milestone.

If no one was listening, and no one was contributing, then this would
be happening. I'm sure we'll reach 20,000 before you know it!

I know that some websites probably have this many visits in a day! AQ
has 10,000 messages on the message boards a month...so we're small
grapes...but that is OK. I'm not trying to be HUGE here...just want
to make sure that the people who want to listen...know about us.

Thanks everyone.

jackbear

10.000. Pretty impressive :-)

I like what we're doing here and I know a lot of people comment on enjoying the podcasts.
I find it a great creative outlet and it's a good educational tool as well.

Long live the Letterpod!!

More letterboxers ought to try recording stuff and submitting them. There are many digital
recording devices. Like I've said in the past, my favorite is the Micromemo. I bought mine
at Marshall's and it just pops on my iPod and it's all set up to record. So easy.

Plus, it has a good sound quality. You can record family members recounting family
history, tape message reminders etc. If anyone's toying with the idea of recording
something, I say go for it. It's lot's of fun!
