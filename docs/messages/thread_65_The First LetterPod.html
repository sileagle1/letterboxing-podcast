I just listened to the letterpodcasts and I just wanted to say how much
I enjoyed them.

Then I listened to a few geocache podcasts. Their broadcast #10 does a
good job of explaining how to record a podcache (audio clues to a
cache). They do a good job with the clues regarding waiting for a
crosswalk light to change.

Also, I believe the first letterboxes that required you to download and
listen to a web-based audio file was Martian Maggot's "Music to My
Ears" (July 14, 2001) and PCLG's "Enigma" (May 6, 2001). Not quite the
same as listening as you go, but similar.
