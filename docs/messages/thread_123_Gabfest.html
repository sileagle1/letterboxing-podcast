From: johnoballou (johnjballou@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-12-05 16:53:07 UTC
Just to be clear for others that might be looking, it's Gabcast (not
Gabfest...that would be the North Carolina board on AQ;-) at
http://www.gabast.com

Yes, the recordings don't come out at the quality of a microphone and
a computer can give you, but it works for things like this when you
have lots of people participating. It's totally free too, which ain't
bad. This is how I have people record an intro for the letterpod if
they have no other means.

I also use something called the Gizmo Project to call people and
record, this works well if you have to have two people interacting. I
can also do conference calling through that as well. If you are
interested its at http://www.gizmoproject.com

OK....let's get recording...we have 11 days left!
