Letterboxing Birthday Party

From: Diana Newton Wood (diana@kjsl.com) | Date: 2006-06-13 08:19:42 UTC-04:00
Well,

I am not sure this whole topic is on topic here, but so far this isn't
a large volume list, so I will reply to Kathie :) Let me know if you
are interested or not in this as a podcast topic for a future podcast.

I have two sons at home still age 6 and 9, and I am having their
birthday party together. I have about 11 acres of conservation
restricted land, and I abut the Great Meadows refuge and the Sudbury
Valley Trustees (who have a box on their property next to me) I have
invited about 30 kids, in both age groups, and mentioned that any
adult that wants to stay is welcome. I have carved and hidden 5 boxes,
which are planned to be planted in the wild after the party.
They are identical to my regular boxes except that I didn't put the
ink and stamp in a bag for now.

First I am going to have them make a stamp. I got the blocks from
Letterbox Adventures, and I got foamie stickers from Michaels. Their
goodie bag is a fanny pack, from Oriental trading, and it will have a
log, compass and ink (not washable because that was too expensive)
They are instructed to wear long pants and play clothes.

Each box is hidden out of site of the others, so no one will see them
being opened. I plan to divide them into 5 groups, age appropriate,
and send each group out with an adult. I may or may not have one set
of clues for each age group. I have a picture clue already for one of
the boxes. I was also planning some decoding, and perhaps clues hidden
along the way. I have some landmarks planted as well (one of those
tree faces, chimes, a dog's grave)

I was going to wait until all groups were back before sending them all
out again. If the older ones were done really early, I could send them
to the Wol-box next door, or even my King Philip's Wood ones at the
end of the street.

I have posted the boxes on Atlasquest so that they can log their
finds, and I will give them a business card, with the urls. I am
hoping some of them will continue after the party!

I was going to have a cookout, but I may not because it is from 1-5 ish.

That was all I had so far-any suggestions welcome, and please if you
don't want to hear any more of this onlist, I can reply off

Diana
DEF Dale End Farm

PS Thanks for the stamp compliment :) It is my 4th since April
Vacation, as I got better carving :)

only compatible with Windows XP. It's the one that I
purchased and works OK.
