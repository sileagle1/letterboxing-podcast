Wish you could go to Maine for the annual Frighnight gathering?

Well maybe you can't make it in person, but you can make it LIVE!

Representatives from the Letterpod (your letterboxing podcast) will be
hosting a LIVE internet broadcast from the scary Moloch Manor in
Gorham, Maine at the Frightnight 4 gathering on Saturday, October 25
from 1-5 p.m.

You can see it LIVE by going to :
http://www.ustream.tv/channel/frightnight4
on October 25th from 1-5 p.m.

You can interact with the hosts via a live chat on the right side of
the screen.

Please stop in and say hello, and see all the crazy letterboxers in
costume!

For more information please go to:
http://letterpod.podbean.com/2008/10/17/live-at-frightnight-4/
