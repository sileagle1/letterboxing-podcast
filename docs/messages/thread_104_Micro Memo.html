From: kathiekelleher (kathiekelleher@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-21 14:57:32 UTC
I recently saw the Micro Memo digital voice recorder (for the iPod) on sale at Marshalls for
$20. I paid $60 for mine, so this is an incredible price.

These devices just click on to the bottom of your iPod and presto, it sets the iPod directly to
record mode. This is what I use and I love the little thing. I've also used it to record family
history and live concerts. The sound quality is amazing. CD quality... Anyone considering
contributing to the Letterpod podcasts, really should head to your local Marshall's to buy this
reasonable priced Micro memo.
