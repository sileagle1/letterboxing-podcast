Okay- so after rereading both Ryan and Mark's adventures to Dartmoor, I nominate that
they both read the narratives of their trips for a future podcast. John or someone else
could give a brief history of letterboxing and then the blog entries. The adventures are
beautifully written and many folks probably haven't found them yet (their loss!). Plus it
would be cool to hear the stories read in Mark and Ryan's own voices.

This is just what I'd consider a natural next step after an introduction. The history of
letterboxing is actually part of the introduction. It should be a two part podcast because it
then needs to follow up with the US history of boxing. The US history would then lead to
everything else. That would definitely fill up some podcast recording time!

Take care, Kathie/Orion
