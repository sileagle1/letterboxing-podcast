Mac makes it pretty easy. Open Garageband, then open New File. When
box comes up, choose New Instrument, and choose Voice. Record voice
using built-in mic if you have nothing else, but a decent mic is worth
the investment. Use headphones to cut out background noise. Then turn
off/uncheck Metronome and do your thing. Save in a file and then you
are ready to edit!

LW
