A podcast can be anything really. A voice over really isn't
necessary, or music. Those things are fairly easy to add if you have

footage, I would totally take it. I don't have much experience with

voice over and music can be a podcast if it is given an RSS feed
(which is what we do here at letterpod...hahaa)
