From: Marissa Dupont (marissa.dupont@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-03-18 21:40:08 UTC-04:00

Have fun jackbear!!! I just got back and had a fantastic time! Definitely take the streetcar (on the corner of Canal and Carondelet) into the Garden District to see all the mansions! And go to the Palace Cafe, the food there is phenomenal. I enjoyed Emeril's Delmonico as well, and Broussard's (in the French Quarter). Go to Cafe Du Monde for beignets (I went twice because they were so good!).

I got the Cafe Du Monde boxes (great!) the Cancer Corner one by Green Tortuga, and Mr. Lee goes to Mardi Gras. Jazz & Blues is missing. Blackberry Patch just posted a list on the LA AQ board about the statuses of the New Orleans-area boxes, definitely print that before you go.

And have a fantastic time!!!!

P.S. I left hitchhikers at the Cafe Du Monde and Mr. Lee, maybe you'll pick them up! :)

-gollygee
