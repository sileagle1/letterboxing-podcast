Rick-in-Boca lamented the lack of boxes planted in New Jersey for
Plant a Letterbox Day, so I'm planting quite a number of Mystery
boxes this week. They'll be up by this weekend.

I like Mystery boxes (I only have one plant that isn't one) because
they tend to be lower traffic, and will likely stay there longer than
those boxes which lead you right to them.

I also like creating puzzles for others to figure out. I used to
play Dungeons and Dragons quite a bit in high school and college, and
I found that I liked being the Dungeon Master more than actually
playing the game. I think I liked the power trip of being in charge
of the situation. Maybe that's why I have almost an equal number of
plants and finds (soon to be plants > finds). I planted my first box
before I found my first. Rick was, incidentally, the first (and
only, so far) finder of my first plant, "Heartbeat Road".
