Well, I'm wrapping up post-production of the Fright Night 4 video. I
think I'm going to follow that up with a bloopers reel since there is
some funny stuff that didn't make the final cut.

The video is longer than years past, just because I couldn't seem to
cut out fun stuff. So, hopefully it isn't too unbearable to watch.
Audio was kind of a disaster, as I was using a new computer I was a
bit unfamilar with, and didn't have the settings right. Hopefully the
material is better than my limitations.

I also plan to have some tales from Oldhounder. I have two great
stories he told me that were too long for the video. Also, I finally
got him and Dale End Farm to tell there 13th Body story, which has
been hinted at since the earliest days of the letterpod.

So, expect more videos in the coming months.

I hope to polish off an audio episode this month, just need a few more
key elements. So, whoever gets me something first, I'm going with that!

Hopefully we still have some listeners out there, since it has been so
long since the last episode.

jackbear
